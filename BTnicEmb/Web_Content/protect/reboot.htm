Reconnection Instructions

Use the Ethernet Discoverer to find BTnic devices on the local network. If you need to restore compile-time settings you can clear the board's EEPROM:
  1. Connect the RESET jumper momentarily to reboot the BTnic.
  2. Reconnect the RESET jumper during the four second bootloader process. The STATUS LED will turn to indicate the EEPROM is being cleared and will turn off when complete.
  3. Remove the RESET jumper. The board will boot with the restored factory default values.
